Pad See Ew Recipe

Pad See Ew is a thick stir fried Thai noodle dish.

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Sen Yai Noodles
  • Chinese Broccoli (Gai Yan)
  • Protein (chicken, egg, or pork)

Preparation:

  1. Cook protein and vegetables first, then remove.
  2. Add noodles and sauce, stir 20 seconds
  3. add chickena and vegetables back in
  4. Enjoy!
