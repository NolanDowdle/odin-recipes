Laab Mhoo Recipe

Laab Mhoo is a flavourful spicy Thai dish.

Ingredients

  • 10oz boneless pork
  • 1/8 cup water
  • 1 tbsp kaffir lime leaf
  • 1 tbsp ground rice
  • 3 shallots or spring onions
  • 1 tsp thai red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp thai fish sauce
  • 1-2 limes juiced
  • 1/2 tbsp of thai chili peppers

Preparation:

  1. Mince the pork. Do not chop too finely, so that the meat retains some texture
  2. Add water to a hot pan and add the minced pork. Cook for 3 minutes stirring continuously.
  3. Add the kaffir lime leaf and the toasted rice. Stir well and turn off the heat
  4. Add chopped shallots or spring onions.
  5. Add dried chili powder and fish sauce and stir well.
  6. Add lime juice and fresh Thai chili peppers and stir
  7. Enjoy!
